Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list an item?

+

Listing items on Price Drop Marketplace is completely free. We don't charge any fees to sellers for listing their items.

Can I stop the price from dropping further?

+

Yes, you set a minimum price when you list your item. The price will never drop below this amount. If the item reaches the minimum price and doesn't sell, the price resets to the original asking price.

How do I know when a price is about to drop?

+

Each item displays a countdown timer showing exactly when the next price drop will occur. You can also set up notifications to alert you when items you're watching reach specific price points.

Can I buy an item immediately without waiting?

+

Absolutely! You can purchase any item at its current price at any time. If you see something you like, you don't have to wait for the price to drop further, as someone else might buy it before you.

What happens if multiple people want to buy at the same time?

+

Items are sold on a first-come, first-served basis. Once a purchase is initiated, the item is temporarily reserved to prevent double-selling. If a purchase fails to complete, the item becomes available again.
